Frequently Asked Questions


Here are some of our most frequently asked questions. Hope they help!


  1. Q: My computer does not turn on, what do I do now?

    A: Make sure it is completely plugged into the wall socket and that the power switch on the plug strip is turned on

  2. Q: I get a 'no signal input' message on my monitor, what do I do?

    A: Verify that the monitor is properly connected in the back of the monitor as well as to the back of the computer.

  3. Q: What do I do if the image on my monitor is distorted or skewed?

    A: A distorted image can be caused by magnetic or other types of interference. Verify no speakers, fans or other magnetic devices are close to the monitor.

  4. Q: How can I clean my keyboard?

    A: Carefully pop the keys off, this will allow you to clean under the keys as well as cleaning the keys themselves.

  5. Q: I am unable to send or receive email?

    A: Verify that your computer is able to see the Internet and/or other computers to ensure that your computer is not encountering a connection issue, which would be causing your e-mail issue.

  6. Q: I can't connect to my network drive anymore, what can I do?

    A: Verify that the network cable is properly connected to the back of the computer.

  7. Q: How do I map a network drive?

    A: Open Microsoft Explorer. From Explorer, click the Tools drop down menu and click the option "Map Network Drive."

  8. Q: How do I determine my computer's name?

    A: Right-click My Computer. Click Properties. In the Properties window, click the Computer Name tab. Within this tab you'll be able to see the full computer name, workgroup and also a description.

  9. Q: What do I do when my computer crashes?

    A: When your computer no longer responds to keyboard commands your best bet is to restart the computer.

  10. Q: What do I do if my hard disk fails to work?

    A: Shut down your computer and restart it.
